# FUTURE_SUPERSTORE-SALES-ANALYSIS_01

# Project Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to analyze the performance of the Super Store by tracking key sales metrics, identifying major revenue drivers, understanding temporal sales trends, and evaluating performance across different customer segments, product categories, regions, and shipping modes. The analysis covers a four-year period (2011, 2012, 2013, and 2014).

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The report summarizes the overall sales health with the following top-line metrics:

**Total Revenue:** 2.30 Million (M)
**Total Orders:** 10 Thousand (K)
**Total Profit:** 286.40 Thousand (K)
**Average Revenue:** 229.86
**Total Quantity:** 38 Thousand (K)
**Total Discount:** 1.56 Thousand (K)

# Key Findings & Insights

# Sales Trends & Performance

**Seasonal Peak:** Sales exhibit a clear seasonal trend, peaking significantly towards the end of the year.
November(0.35M)andDecember(0.33M) are the strongest months for revenue, while February ($0.06M) is the lowest.

# Product & Category Analysis

**Dominant Categories:** Technology is the leading revenue category at 36.4%, closely followed by Furniture at 32.3% and Office Supplies at 31.3%. The revenue distribution across categories is relatively balanced.

**Top Sellers:** The top-performing sub-categories are Phones(0.33M)andChairs(0.33M), generating the highest revenue.

# Customer & Regional Analysis

**Leading Segment:** The Consumer segment is the largest revenue contributor, accounting for 50.56% of total sales, followed by the Corporate (30.74%) and Home Office (18.7%) segments.

**Top Regions:** The West region leads in total revenue with 725.46K,Eastregion(678.78K). Central and South regions are the lower contributors.

# Operational Metrics

**Shipping Preference:** Standard Class is the overwhelming preference for shipping, accounting for 59.12% of all revenue by ship mode. Second Class is the next most common at 19.99%.

# Report Components & Functionality

The dashboard provides interactive filters for Year, State, City, Ship Mode, and Segment, allowing users to drill down into specific data cuts. Visualizations include:
Bar Chart: Revenue Trend by Month.

**Donut Charts:** Revenue distribution by Category, Segment, and Ship Mode.

**Bar Charts:** Total Revenue by Region and Total Revenue by Top 5 Sub Category.

# Business Impact

This report drives business impact by providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making and operational efficiency:

**Product Focus:**  Validates investment in high-performing products like Technology (36.4%), Phones, and Chairs to maximize sales and optimize inventory.

**Targeted Marketing:** Confirms the Consumer segment (50.56%) as the top priority, allowing for focused marketing campaigns and resource allocation.

**Geographic Strategy:** Highlights the West region ($725.46K) as the primary revenue generator, guiding resource deployment and expansion efforts.

**Operational Readiness:** The clear seasonal peak in November/December allows for proactive planning in inventory, staffing, and logistics to handle peak demand.

**Logistics Efficiency:**  The dominance of Standard Class (59.12%) shipping informs carrier negotiations and service level evaluation.
